ABOUT
Carbon is a price-stable cryptocurrency with instantaneous rebasement.

Utilizing Keynesian economic theory through a method we’ve developed called “wrapping”, Carbon algorithmically adjusts the supply circulating instantaneously based on AI scalar prediction models. Carbon’s AI protocol can respond to any number of arbitrary changes in demand. Markov-chain optimization and markovian decision processes determine real-time incentives to make sure these incentives always match market conditions.



Carbon has instantaneous monetary contraction/expansion via "wrapping".


Carbon has mathematically-proven fair timestamping monetary policy.


Carbon stablecoins have transaction throughput 300x of Visa.


Carbon stablecoins have high fault tolerance via the Carbon Reserve.
