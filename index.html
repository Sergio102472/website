ABOUT
Prizm is Ethereum's First Staking Stablecoin that allows anyone to trustlessly mint pegged ERC20 stablecoins on the Ethereum Blockchain. These stablecoins are guaranteed by a Proof of Reserve smart contract that can be publically verified by anyone, anywhere, anytime.



Prizm allows stablecoin users to earn trustless interest via staking.


Prizm ensures long term price stability using decentralized monetary policy.


Stablecoins can be sent to anyone, anywhere with an ERC20 wallet.


Prizm protocol amendments are governed by a DAO with checks & balances.
