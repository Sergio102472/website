ABOUT
Prizm is Ethereum's First Decentralized Staking Stablecoin Network (DSSN) that allows anyone to trustlessly mint custom-pegged ERC20 stablecoins on the Ethereum Blockchain. These stablecoins are guaranteed by a Proof of Reserve smart contract that can be publically verified by anyone, anywhere, anytime.


Prizm allows long term investors to earn trustless interest on any deposit.

Prizm enables cryptoasset holders to mint custom-pegged stablecoins.

Stablecoins can be sent to anyone with an ERC20 wallet.

Prizm ensures long term price stability using macroeconomic incentivizes.
